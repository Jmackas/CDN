Our Organisation

Wherever you are based in the world we can reach you. We provide leading-edge, distance learning which is live and interactive.

Our programs are best suited to students working in industry who require practical, relevant education that is delivered efficiently and flexibly. The online techniques include the live delivery of lectures, remote laboratories and simulation applications.

Our range of training and education solutions are comprehensive and allow students to move between the vocational and higher education sectors as required.

Students can register for Professional Certificates of Competency or for accredited qualifications ranging from Diplomas, through to Advanced Diplomas, Bachelors of Science, Graduate Certificates, Graduate Diplomas and Master Degrees in engineering and technology.
