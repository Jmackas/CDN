Step 1

Navigate to the tab Login/Register.

Step 2

When on that page, select “Register as a Student”.

Step 3

Fill in the details, and then select "Create my account"

.

Step 4

Navigate to a course you wish to purchase.

Step 5

Select 'Take This Course'.

Step 6

You will be redirected to your cart, where you can either enter a promo code, or just select 'Checkout'.

You will then be redirected to PayPal.

Step 7

You can either pay with a PayPal account, or simply pay with a card via the button 'Pay with a Card'.
